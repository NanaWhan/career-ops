# Loom Script Template

Loom video introductions follow a 3-beat structure. Keep it under 90 seconds.
Save every script you write here so you build a reusable library.

---

## The 3-Beat Structure

**Beat 1 — Hook (5–10 seconds)**
> One sentence that makes them want to keep watching. Not "Hi I'm Cherish." Lead with the most interesting thing about you or what you're about to show them.

Example:
> "I built a government platform that serves 100,000 people a day. Here's why I want to apply that at [Company]."

**Beat 2 — Proof (40–60 seconds)**
> The one project or achievement most relevant to this specific role. Keep it specific — metrics, stack, constraints. Don't list everything. Pick the one thing that would make this team want to talk to you.

**Beat 3 — Close (10–15 seconds)**
> Why this company, why now. One concrete thing about the role or product that you care about. Then a direct ask.

Example:
> "I'd love to bring that to [Company]'s [specific product/team]. Looking forward to the conversation."
